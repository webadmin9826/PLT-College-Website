The Brigthest Star Shines at PLTCI

PLT College, Inc. (PLTCI) was established in 1991 with a vision of being an icon in humane education, to develop professionally competent, socially responsible, and God-centered individuals. PLTCI provides various educational services in the realm of health, business, and technology, with a team of dedicated professionals willing to deliver excellent customer service for the general public. It is a non-stock, non-profit, and non-sectarian educational institution.
